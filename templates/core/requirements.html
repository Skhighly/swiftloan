Loan Requirements

Everything you need to qualify for a SwiftLoan — transparent and upfront.

Eligibility Criteria
Kenyan Citizen or Resident

Must hold a valid National ID card.

Valid M-Pesa Account

Active Safaricom M-Pesa number registered in your name.

Valid KRA PIN

A valid Kenya Revenue Authority Personal Identification Number.

Employment / Income Source

Must have a verifiable job or income source. Your qualified amount depends on your monthly income.

Age 18+

You must be at least 18 years of age to apply.

Application Process
1
Create Account

Register with your email address and set a password.

2
Submit Personal Info

Provide your full legal name and phone number.

3
Upload National ID

Photo of both the front and back of your National ID card (max 5MB each).

4
Enter M-Pesa & KRA PIN

Your active Safaricom M-Pesa number and KRA PIN for identity verification.

5
Job Details

Job title, employer name, and monthly income — used to determine your qualified amount.

6
Select Loan Amount & Apply

Choose an amount up to your qualified limit and state your reason for applying.

7
Wait for Approval (5 min)

Our system auto-reviews your application. You'll be notified via the Notifications tab.

8
Pay Deposit via M-Pesa

Pay the refundable 10% deposit to unlock your loan. Funds are disbursed to your M-Pesa within 24 hours.

Qualification Tiers

Your qualified amount is based on monthly income.

Monthly Income Max Loan Deposit
Under KES 10,000 KES 3,000 KES 300
KES 10,000 – 24,999 KES 5,000 KES 500
KES 25,000 – 49,999 KES 10,000 KES 1,000
KES 50,000 and above KES 20,000 KES 2,000
